The Ancient Grains
The Millet Story Board
Before rice dominated our plates, millets fed civilisations. Here is the story of each grain — and why your family deserves them back.
📜 A Story 5,000 Years in the Making

Long before wheat and rice became staples, millets were the superfoods of ancient India. Telugu farmers grew them in their fields. Grandmothers cooked them every morning. Children grew up strong on ragi laddo and jowar roti.

Then, somewhere along the way, we forgot them. We traded our ancestral nutrition for convenience — and paid for it with our health. Nelavanka Foods exists to reverse that. One pack at a time, one family at a time, we are bringing millets back to where they belong — your kitchen table.

🌾
Ragi — Finger Millet
కొర్రలు · The Calcium King
Most Used

Ragi has been the backbone of South Indian kitchens for centuries. Our grandmothers ground it fresh every morning — into porridge for babies, laddos for festivals, and rotis for farmers who worked the fields from dawn to dusk. It was the first food and the last medicine.

Key Benefits
🦴 Highest calcium of any grain
🩸 Manages blood sugar naturally
💪 Builds strong bones in children
⚖️ Excellent for weight management
🧠 Reduces anxiety and stress
👶 Best first food for babies

Jowar — Sorghum
జొన్నలు · The Digestion Healer
Gluten Free

Jowar was the grain of Deccan farmers — grown in dry lands, requiring no irrigation, feeding entire communities through droughts. It is one of the most sustainable and nutritious grains ever cultivated. Our ancestors knew this. Modern science is just catching up.

Key Benefits
🌿 100% gluten-free
💚 Heals gut and digestion
❤️ Reduces bad cholesterol
🔋 Sustained energy all day
💧 Keeps you full longer
🌱 High in antioxidants

Bajra — Pearl Millet
సజ్జలు · The Warming Grain
Iron Rich

Bajra is the grain of Rajasthan and Andhra's hot, dry regions — a warrior grain that grows where nothing else can. It generates warmth in the body, making it perfect for winter mornings. Farmers who ate bajra roti worked all day without fatigue. That same energy now lives in every Nelavanka bajra product.

Key Benefits
🩸 Fights anaemia — very high iron
🔥 Generates body warmth
💪 High in magnesium
❤️ Protects heart health
🌾 Best for pregnant women
⚡ Instant energy booster
Our Products: Bajra Murukulu · Bajra Upma Premix · Multi Millet Upma Premix · Multi Millet Ladoo

Korra — Foxtail Millet
కొర్రలు · The Blood Sugar Balancer
Diabetic Friendly

Korra is considered the most nutritionally balanced of all millets. Andhra households have been cooking korra pongal, korra rice, and korra khichdi for thousands of years. It is the grain most recommended by modern nutritionists for diabetics — because it releases energy slowly and keeps blood sugar steady.

Key Benefits
🍬 Best grain for diabetics
🧠 Rich in B-vitamins
💚 Improves immunity
🫀 Lowers bad cholesterol
🌿 High in dietary fibre
✨ Glows the skin naturally
Our Products: Foxtail Murukulu · Foxtail Sev · Millet Hot Pongal · Little Millets Khichdi Premix

Kodo Millet — Varagu
అరికలు · The Detox Grain
Rare Grain

Kodo millet is one of the rarest and most powerful grains in Ayurvedic tradition. Ancient texts recommend it for detoxification, liver health, and managing obesity. It was the grain reserved for fasting days in Telugu households — because it cleanses while nourishing. At Nelavanka, we are proud to be one of the very few brands bringing Kodo back to your plate.

Key Benefits
🧹 Powerful body detox
🫀 Supports liver function
⚖️ Best for weight loss
🩸 Manages blood pressure
💤 Improves sleep quality
🌾 Very high in fibre
Our Products: Kodo Millet Almond Cookies · Kodo Jantikalu

Samalu — Little Millet
సామలు · The Gut Guardian
High Fibre

Samalu is the smallest millet but packs the biggest nutritional punch per gram. Used in traditional Telugu fasting foods, it is considered sacred and pure. Grandmothers cooked samalu khichdi for the sick because it heals the gut and restores strength gently. Today it is the foundation of our beloved Millet Hot Pongal.

Key Benefits
🌿 Heals gut lining
🍽️ Very easy to digest
🦠 Rich in zinc — boosts immunity
📉 Lowers triglycerides
🧒 Safe for toddlers
💧 Keeps body cool
